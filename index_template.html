Chao Xu | 许(xǔ) 超(chāo)

I'm an Assistant Professor at UESTC, a member of the Algorithms and Logic Group. My research interests are algorithms, combinatorial optimization and computational geometry. I'm also interested in more applied problems with nice theoretical components. Here is my CV(last updated October 2021).

I have worked at Voleon, Grab and Google as a software engineer, and was a research scientist at Yahoo! Research in New York.

I obtained my PhD in Computer Science from University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign in May 2018. My advisors were Karthik Chandrasekaran and Chandra Chekuri. I finished my undergrad in mathematics at Stony Brook University in 2013, and spent Spring 2012 in Budapest Semesters in Mathematics.

I maintain a github account, a blog and a Mastodon @chao. I frequent cstheory.

You can contact me through email cxu@uestc.edu.cn.
